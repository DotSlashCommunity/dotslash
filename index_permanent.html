But, Why?
A website development tool provides companies with a podium for this necessary web presence and allows them to get in touch with millions of web surfers all over the world, widening the range of their potential customers. This doesn't just make the companies accessible, but accessible to the global population. It also gives businesses the chance to portray themselves in the ways they want to be seen, with a platform that's built on their terms and can effectively market the services or products that they offer. In addition, a website will make businesses look more reliable if they're represented in a professional way. To develop a website in accordance with specific criteria, the business can either hire a developer or do it themselves by choosing from the best web development software, which will assist in creating polished websites. Website development can include anything from simply programming the data of a website to adding client liaison, content, network security and web server configuration, e-commerce development and more. It usually ranges from creating the simplest, plain text to the most difficult web-based applications, social network services and electronic business. A great way to reinforce the personal recommendations of other clients is through professional website development.
Contents?
The current talk will be aimed to introduce you with the technologies you might end up using regularly while on your journey of Web Development. The plan is to showcase a product developed in-house by the DotSlash Community and then go on and talk about all the design choices and technologies used during the entire process, we believe this will help you with understanding the components and integrations that make up an actual and functional WebApp. Anyways, here is a broad plan of what we will be talking about:
  • Web Stack Technologies in General
  • Basics of Front-End Development (HTML, CSS, JS)
  • Backends Systems, Integration flows with Front Ends
Prerequisites
All we expect from you is your ability to turn the computer on.
Carrying a laptop is optional though recommened.
